# Data-Analysis-Class
## Ice-Breaker Prompts
Take turns asking your peer these questions. Assign a peer number to yourself and your peer: **PEER 1 & PEER 2**

## 🌍 Global & Culturally Prompts
#### PEER 1 ASKS: 
•	**Local Weather:** What does the weather look like outside your window right now?
•	**Commute Savings:** Do you commute to any work or school? Are ADMI’s online classes helping you save on commute time and money? 
•	**Morning Ritual:** What is the very first thing you do when you wake up?

#### PEER 2 ASKS: 
•	**Weekend Recharge:** How do you usually spend your time to disconnect on days off?
•	**Local Snack:** What is a popular comfort food or snack from your current city?
•	**Language Share:** What is the most spoken language in your country?
• **Religious Share:** What’s the most common religion in your area?

## 💻 Digital & Desktop 
#### PEER 1 ASKS:
•	**Tab Count:** How many browser tabs do you have open right now?

#### PEER 2 ASKS:
•	**Desktop Cleanliness:** Be Honest. Don’t cheat. Is your computer desktop perfectly organized, or completely cluttered with files?

## 🌱 Lifestyle, Hobbies & Routine
#### PEER 1 ASKS:
•	**Skill Upgrade:** What is a practical skill are you/have you been mastering this year?
•	**Book or Screen:** Do you prefer reading physical books, using an e-reader, or listening to audiobooks?
•	**Window View:** If you look outside right now, do you see more nature or more buildings?

#### PEER 2 ASKS:
•	**Daily Beverage:** What is your exact go-to drink to get through the workday? (tea, water, coffee, other)
• **Activity Level:** Do you prefer to relax by exercising or by resting quietly?
•	**Cooking Habit**: Do you prefer following a strict recipe, or do you just guess the ingredients?
